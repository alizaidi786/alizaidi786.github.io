About Us

Vishwa Shia Parishad is a worldwide shia origanization which was founded by Raz Haider Zaidi in the year 2015 in India.

We are globally connected to all the shia asna ashri who are interested in this organization. This is a non profit organization which mainly focus on the issues of shias at international level.

It was found by keeping the context in mind that it would help to all the shias in the world. But the supporters aren't enough to keep it on the roll. Hence we are requesting each and every individual to help the organization financially, physically and by being the active member of the organization.

We have been doing so many programs for the shia society. We are less in numbers but our faith is more stronger than any sub-caste in Islam.

We are also politically active so that we could give every possible support to our society when we are in need.
